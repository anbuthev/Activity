How to Play:

  1. Click "Start Game" to go to the Wheel Spinner.
  2. Spin the wheel to choose a team member to play.
  3. Click "Go to Word Search Game" to begin your turn.
  4. Find all hidden team member names in the grid horizontally, vertically, or diagonally within 1 minute (01:00)!
  5. Your score will be tracked and updated automatically on the scoreboard.
